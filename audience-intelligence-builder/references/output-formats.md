```markdown
**Who they listen to:**
- [Types of people / specific thought leaders they follow and why]

- [Publications, newsletters, podcasts, communities — be specific]

- [2-3 proof points that earn trust. Specific results, named examples, data from reputable sources, vulnerability about failures.]

---

*Designed to be uploaded alongside source content to tailor outputs for this audience.*
```

**Writing guidelines for content mode:**
- Be specific, not generic. "They care about ROI" is useless. A data point with a source is useful.
- Include data inline — don't separate into a references section.
- Use their language in the profile itself. If they say "CAC", write "CAC".
- Keep it to one page. Cut the weakest claims if it runs long. Density beats length.
- Prioritize actionable insights over descriptive ones.

**Writing guidelines for marketing mode:**
- Research-backed: every major claim needs a source
- Specific and detailed — no vague generalities; concrete behaviours and traits
- Actionable: clear guidance on how to reach and convert this persona
- Use third person about the persona ("Sarah values…" not "You value…")
- Never rely solely on assumptions — always start with external research

## Delivery

**Content mode:**
1. Save to `skills/linkedin-post-workflow/references/audience-profiles/<audience-name>.md`
2. Present using the canvas or paste inline
3. Brief summary: which sections had the strongest research, flag areas where data was thin

**Marketing mode:**
1. Save to `research/personas/<audience-name>.md`
2. Present inline
3. Brief summary: strongest-evidenced sections, any gaps to validate with first-party data

---

## Edge Cases

- **Very niche audience:** Do your best with available data. Clearly flag where you're extrapolating from broader data and suggest the user validate.
- **Broad audience (e.g. "marketers"):** Push back. "Marketers" is too broad. Ask them to pick a specific role and seniority level.
- **Missing one input:** Ask for it before starting. Both inputs are needed.
- **Multiple profiles requested:** Create separate files per audience. Each is self-contained.
