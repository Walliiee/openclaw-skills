# AEO & AI Search (Dharmesh Perspective)

Answer Engine Optimization strategy from Dharmesh Shah's experience at HubSpot and the My First Million podcast.

---

## The Distribution Shift

Every major distribution shift creates huge growth opportunity:
- Early Facebook ads → massive businesses
- Early blogging/SEO → organic traffic competitors still fight for
- **Now:** AI Chat Applications (ChatGPT: 800M weekly users, plus Claude, Perplexity, Gemini)

**SEO traffic has declined 20-40% across industries** — including HubSpot, which played the SEO game exceptionally well for years.

### Why: Behavior Changed
- Old: Google → 10 blue links → click through → piece together answer
- New: ChatGPT → ask question → get answer immediately → no clicking required

### The Binary Outcome Problem
- Either you show up in the AI's answer, or you **might as well not exist**
- No "page 2" to fall back on
- If ChatGPT mentions 4 CRMs for small businesses, those 4 get all visibility. Everyone else = zero.
- Click-through rates lower, but intent is higher when people do click

## 6 Tactics for AEO Optimization

### 1. Enable AI Crawling
- Don't block OpenAI SearchBot and GPT bots
- If blocking unknown crawlers by default, explicitly whitelist AI bots
- Basic but many companies miss it — "you can't win if you're not in the race"

### 2. Track AI Traffic
- Google Analytics and HubSpot now show AI referral traffic as separate source
- Measure your baseline today
- Many companies already get meaningful AI traffic without realizing it

### 3. Reframe Content Structure
**Most important change:** Shift from narrative blog posts → structured question-answer formats
- Make it easy for AI to extract clear answers
- Lead with definitive recommendations and primary factors
- Hooks designed for human readers matter less than parseable structure

### 4. Apply the "Deserve to Rank" Test
- For any topic, ask ChatGPT that exact question right now
- Look at what it cites
- Ask: "Do I have a webpage that honestly deserves to rank higher?"
- If not, **create that content first** — AI systems reward genuinely better answers

### 5. Leverage Classic SEO Best Practices
How ChatGPT works behind the scenes:
1. Converts your question into search queries
2. Processes search results
3. Generates answer using LLM

**Therefore:** Page authority, quality backlinks, and technical optimization still matter — they get you into the initial result set ChatGPT analyzes.

### 6. Invest in Reddit & Social Discussions
- AI systems heavily index Reddit threads with clear Q&A + upvotes
- Particularly valuable for software/service recommendations
- Provide thoughtful, genuine answers in relevant discussions

## Dharmesh's AEO Experiments

1. **Cross-posting to LinkedIn** — Testing if high-engagement LinkedIn newsletter articles get picked up by ChatGPT/Perplexity (LinkedIn has strong domain authority)
2. **Content format testing** — Restructuring to Q&A oriented, leading with clear takeaways
3. **Measurement & iteration** — Tracking AI referral traffic and correlating with content changes
